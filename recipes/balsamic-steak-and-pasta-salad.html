Balsamic Steak and Pasta Salad

Description

This high protein steak & pasta salad recipe is perfect as a main dish or a side at your next bbq! It is also easy enough to make a large batch on meal prep Sunday to enjoy throughout the week!

Ingredients

  • 1lb Sirloin Tip Steak
  • 8oz Banza High Protein Pasta, cooked and drained
  • 4/3 cup asparagus, cut
  • 1 cup sliced cherry tomatoes
  • 4 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 1/2 cup goat cheese
  • Garlic powder, salt & pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Slice steak into thin strips and grill until desired doneness
  2. Toss together cooked pasta, asparagus, tomato, balsamic, and seasonings
  3. Add in cooked steak and goat cheese
  4. Toss gently
  5. Serve immediately or store in a sealed container until ready to enjoy!
